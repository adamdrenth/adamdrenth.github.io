Ice Cream Float

by: Adam Drenth

This is my favourite recipe. It is a delicious snack for movie.
Some people may even call it a "spider".

Ingredients

  • 1 glass
  • 1 can ginger ale
  • 1 scoop of vanilla ice cream
  • 1 spoon
  • 1 straw

Directions

  1. Put ice cream in the glass
  2. Pour in ginger ale
  3. Add spoon and straw
  4. Enjoy!
