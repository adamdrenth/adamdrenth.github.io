4 Ingredient Peanut Butter Cookies

By: Hannah Noll

This recipe is quick and easy to make! It's perfect for parties, a movie night, or just a fun treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 bowl
  • 1 spoon
  • 1 fork
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of peanut butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325 degrees
  2. Mix all of the ingredients in the bowl until fully combined
  3. Shape the dough into 1 inch balls
  4. Place the balls one inch apart on an ungreased baking sheet
  5. Flatten the balls gently with a fork 2 times (horizontally and vertically) to make criss-cross impressions
  6. Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven for 15 minutes or until golden brown

ENJOY!
