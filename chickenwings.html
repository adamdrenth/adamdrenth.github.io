Chicken Wings Recipe

By: Someone as inexperienced as you

Here's an easy recipe for all cooking newbies. Don't mess up

Ingredients

  • 10 chicken wings
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3 cloves pressed garlic
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 1 hour
  1. Preheat oven to 190 degrees Celcius
  2. Place garlic, garlic powder, olive oil, chili powder, salt, and pepper in ziploc bag, shake
  3. Place wings in bag, re-shake
  4. Arrange on baking sheet, cook for 1 hour
  5. Watches Youtube videos while waiting
  6. Cool and Enjoy!!
